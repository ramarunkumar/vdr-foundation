Welcome to VDR FOUNDATION

VDR FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the welfare and empowerment of cleanliness workers, differently abled individuals, and senior citizens. We strive to create an inclusive and compassionate society where every individual can live with dignity and respect.

Read More

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where everyone, regardless of their background or abilities, has equal access to opportunities and resources.

Our Mission

To empower cleanliness workers, differently abled individuals, and senior citizens through targeted programs that improve their quality of life and foster social inclusion.

Our Values

Dignity, Respect, Inclusion, Compassion, and Social Responsibility.

Our Focus Areas

Social Inclusion

Creating an inclusive society by providing platforms and opportunities for cleanliness workers, differently-abled individuals, and senior citizens to be active participants in their communities.

Healthcare and Wellness

Ensuring access to healthcare services, promoting physical and mental well-being for marginalized groups who face barriers to essential services.

Skill Development and Employment

Offering vocational training and skill development programs to enhance employability and create economic independence for underserved communities.

Advocacy and Awareness

Challenging societal stigma and raising awareness to foster respect, equality, and dignity for all individuals, regardless of their background or abilities.
