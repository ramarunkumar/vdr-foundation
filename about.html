About VDR FOUNDATION

Committed to dignity, inclusion, and care for all

Who We Are

VDR FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the welfare and empowerment of cleanliness workers, differently abled individuals, and senior citizens. We aim to create an inclusive and compassionate society where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, and a dignified life.

Cleanliness workers form the backbone of our communities, yet they often face social stigma, unsafe working conditions, and limited access to essential services. Differently abled individuals deserve equal opportunities and accessibility, while senior citizens need care, respect, and emotional support.

At VDR FOUNDATION, we address these challenges through education, skill development, healthcare support, awareness campaigns, and community engagement initiatives.

Our Vision

A society where cleanliness workers and differently abled individuals thrive with dignity, and senior citizens live with respect, care, and security.

Our Mission

To empower cleanliness workers and differently abled individuals through education, skill development, and employment opportunities, while providing holistic support and care to senior citizens.

Our Goals

Cleanliness Workers
  • Education & skill development
  • Fair wages and welfare benefits
  • Improved working conditions
  • Social & economic empowerment
Differently Abled Individuals
  • Accessible education & training
  • Employment opportunities
  • Entrepreneurship support
  • Disability resource center
Senior Citizens
  • Healthcare & nutrition support
  • Emotional & social engagement
  • Day care & support groups
  • Rights & entitlement advocacy

Our Objectives

  • Establish vocational training centers
  • Launch cleanliness and inclusion awareness campaigns
  • Create job partnerships with organizations
  • Develop senior citizens’ support networks
  • Provide scholarships for students with disabilities
  • Establish a dedicated helpline
