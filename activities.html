Recent initiatives making a difference

2025-2026

Educational Assistance to Hill Tribes

Educational assistance was provided to the children of the hill tribes and agricultural inputs necessary for the livelihood of the hill tribes were provided. This initiative aims to empower tribal communities through education and sustainable agricultural practices.

Education Livelihood Hill Tribes

Coconut Saplings Distribution

Coconut saplings were distributed to hill tribes to support their livelihood and promote sustainable agriculture. This initiative helps families establish long-term income sources while contributing to environmental conservation.

Agriculture Sustainability Livelihood

Food Provided to Hill Tribes

Essential food supplies were provided to hill tribes in need, ensuring nutritional support for vulnerable communities. This program addresses immediate food security while building long-term resilience.
