Bag Donation Initiative

Supporting Government School Students Through Education

VDR Foundation proudly distributed school bags and classroom supplies to students at the Government Primary School in Vadamalapatti. Our goal is to build confidence, encourage attendance, and support learning for every child.

Support the Initiative

About the Initiative

VDR Foundation worked with local leaders and educators to bring a meaningful bag donation program to students in Tenkasi district.

In early June 2026, VDR Foundation organized a school bag donation event at the Government Primary School in Vadamalapatti, Alangulam Taluk, Tenkasi District. The program provided new school bags.

The event was led by VDR Foundation Founder President Mr. R. Velmurugan and supported by education leaders and school staff. Chief guests included Mr. R. Mariappan, District Education Officer; Mrs. V. Sasila, Head Teacher; Mr. C. Devindra, Assistant Teacher; Mr. U. Devanthiran, Assistant Teacher; and Mrs. Vijaya, School Management Committee Leader.

Students, parents and community members joined the celebration, making the bag distribution event a memorable step toward improving educational access and supporting equal opportunity for every child.
